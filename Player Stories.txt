Placement & Movement:

As Player 1, I want to easily place my circles on empty spaces.
As Player 2, I want to easily place my circles on empty spaces.
As a player, I want clear feedback when I place or move a circle.
As a player, I want to know how many circles I have left.
As a player, I want to easily select and move my placed circles.
As a player, I don't want to move circles illegally (diagonally, or onto occupied spaces).

Winning & Game End:

As a player, I want the game to automatically detect and clearly indicate a win (3 in a row).
As a player, I want a clear message indicating the winner.
As a player, I want to easily start a new game.
As a player, I want to be prevented from moving after the game ends.

General:

As a player, I want a clean and intuitive interface.
As a player, I want the game to run smoothly.
As a player, I want clear instructions.
